Ops Web Browser
Ops Web Browser is an online shop for computer technology and office supplies, catering to government institutions and businesses.

Purpose
To provide a platform for government agencies and businesses to procure computer technology and office supplies efficiently.

Features
- Online catalog of products
- Tender submission and management
- Secure payment processing
- Responsive design for ease of use

Goals
- Streamline procurement processes for government agencies and businesses
- Provide a user-friendly platform for customers to browse and purchase products

Target Audience
Government institutions, businesses, and organizations seeking computer technology and office supplies.
